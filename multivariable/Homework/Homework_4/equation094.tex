\frac{\partial f}{\partial x_i}(\overrightarrow{a}) =
\frac{1}{\sqrt{a_{1}^{2} + ... + a_{n}^{2}}}
\cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial a_i} (a_{1}^{2} + ... + a_{n}^{2}) =
\frac{1}{\sqrt{a_{1}^{2} + ... + a_{n}^{2}}} \cdot 2a_i =
\frac{2a_i}{\sqrt{a_{1}^{2} + ... + a_{n}^{2}}} =
\frac{2a_i}{\|\overrightarrow{a}\|}
