Exam II Review

Created Sunday 12 April 2020

In preparation for the second exam, I am making some extra notes. All exams are cumulative so please refer to pages Day 11 and Day 12 for answers to the previous exam.

Exam Notes Cliffnotes

A synopsis of notes Gross gave us about the upcoming Exam II.

  • Exam shall be closed book. By this, he says few questions can be answered right from our notes or assigned reading.
  • Keep a pdf of the exam open in another window to help you answer questions.
  • Know how to edit an email reply so that you only insert question answers in the available space without modifying the original text.
  • To review, read through your class notes and skim the assigned reading.
  • Look over the first exam
  • Look over the exam instructions here.
  • Exam times are the same as you would have for regular class (so for us, we start at 11am).
  • Papers should be graded by next tuesday
  • A video with correct answers will be available Thursday

Online Exam Instructions

The following are notes derived directly from the exam instructions he gave us here.

  • You will get an email with the exam a few minutes before 11am. You must submit, return by email, your exam by 12pm.
  • You will receive two copies of the exam. One is fully formatted as a PDF and the other is a plain text version as the body text of the email.
  • To take and submit your exam, edit the plain text version of the exam to include answers to your questions. Don't email your response as an attachment. Also, don't edit the original text.
  • Do not change the subject line of the email!
  • He will prepare a video with answers on Thursday. Don't bother asking for answers.

Anything up to the videos on graphics is fair game. Also the readings.
